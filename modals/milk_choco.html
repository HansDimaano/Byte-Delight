Milk Choco Cookie Frappe

This delicious Milk Choco Cookie Frappe is the perfect way to cool down on a hot day! Filled with Coffee, ice, and everything nice! Comes with whipped cream too!

Beginner Friendly

Ingredients

  • Café Frappe from Frio (Cold Beverage Solution from NESCAFÉ®) 200 ml
  • Monin Chocolate Cookie Syrup 10 ml
  • Ice Cubes 2-3
  • Chocolate Cookie Bits, Cocoa Powder To Garnish (Optional)
  • Whipped Cream

Instructions

  • Dispense 200 ml of Café Frappe (from Frio) in a glass. If you don't have Frio, you can make this on your own with our Cold Coffee Premix - NESCAFÉ® Ice. Refer instructions here.
  • Add 10 ml of Monin Chocolate Cookie Syrup to the glass.
  • Top up with 2-3 ice cubes.
  • Stir for a couple of seconds
  • Top with whipped cream and serve chilled.

Serving Suggestions:

  • To add some extra punch, garnish with chocolate cookie bits and cocoa powder.
